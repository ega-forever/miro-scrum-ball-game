The Magic Scrum balls Game is a great opportunity to introduce people to basic scrum principles and values in a fun and all-senses involving way.
It simulates a scrum production process and based on the following principles: plan-perform-adapt.
The team will self-organize and form a process based on the rules provided.
The objective is to pass as many balls as possible in the given timeboxes through the team by following certain rules.
Dropped balls reduce as "defects" the final result.
